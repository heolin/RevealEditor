Principles of Clarity

Writing that respects the reader

1 — Lead with the point

Put the conclusion first. Detail earns its place afterward.

2 — Cut the throat-clearing

“It is important to note that…” — delete it and start at the note.

3 — One idea per sentence

If it needs a comma-splice, it needs a full stop.

4 — Prefer the concrete

“Ship it Friday” beats “target an end-of-week deliverable.”
